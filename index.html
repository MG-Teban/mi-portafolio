About

I'm an IT professional based in Perth, Australia, with over 5 years of experience across technical support and identity security. My journey spans multiple industries — giving me a perspective that goes beyond the technical and into the human side of IT.

My specialty is Identity & Access Management: controlling who has access to what, and making sure that access is earned, monitored, and revoked cleanly. I believe IAM is where security either holds or breaks — and I take that seriously.
